About Cool Pop Crew

Cool Pop Crew is a family-run business started by a group of passionate cousins who wanted to bring joy and refreshment to our community. What began as a fun summer project has grown into a beloved local brand known for quality and flavor.

Our Story

We started making ice lollipops in our family kitchen, experimenting with different flavors and recipes. Our goal was simple: create the most delicious, refreshing treats that bring smiles to people's faces. Today, we're proud to serve our community with premium ice lollipops made with care and quality ingredients.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves a sweet moment of happiness. That's why we're committed to providing affordable, high-quality ice lollipops that taste amazing and cool you down on hot days. We source the best ingredients and put love into every pop we make.
